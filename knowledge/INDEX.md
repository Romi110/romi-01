## Adding New Files

Use `@prompts/deep-dive.md [topic]` to generate a new wiki-style knowledge file. The prompt handles placement, depth, and proposes sub-topic files for future exploration.

New domains get new subfolders (e.g., `knowledge/farming/`, `knowledge/skills/`).

## Status Key
- **Active reference** — used regularly, kept up to date
- **Active — ongoing** — being written or extended
- **Reference** — stable, not frequently updated
- **Draft** — initial notes, not complete
