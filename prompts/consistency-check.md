# Consistency Check

Read my context from `context/about-me.md`. Then read the daily entries from the last 7-14 days in the `daily/` folder.

My biggest challenge is consistency. I know this about myself. I need you to be honest with me.

## Your Job

1. **Streak report** — How many of the last 7 days have entries? How many of those are actually filled in vs. mostly blank?

2. **What's slipping?** — Which sections am I consistently skipping? Learning? Reading? Body? Reflection? Name them.

3. **What's working?** — What AM I showing up for? Reinforce that.

4. **Pattern analysis** — Am I fading at a particular point in the week? Are weekends harder? Do I start strong on Monday and die by Thursday?

5. **Stoic reframe** — Remind me: I don't need to feel motivated. I need to decide. Every day I show up, even badly, is better than a perfect plan I abandoned.

6. **One adjustment** — Suggest ONE small change to my routine or approach that might help with the specific pattern you're seeing. Not a system overhaul — one tweak.

## Tone
Honest. Not harsh, not gentle. Like a friend who actually tells you the truth. I asked for this check because I want to stay accountable. Respect that by being real with me.
