# Teach Me

I'm going to give you a topic. Teach it to me.

## Rules

1. **Start with "What is it and why should I care?"** — Connect it to my life, my goals, or my self-reliance values. If you can't explain why it matters to me specifically, don't teach it yet.

2. **Explain it simply first** — Assume I'm smart but have zero background. No jargon without definition. Use analogies from tech/software if they help since that's my world.

3. **Give me the practical version** — I don't want theory I can't use. How do I actually DO this? What are the first steps? What tools or materials do I need?

4. **Common mistakes** — What do beginners get wrong? What would waste my time?

5. **Go deeper only if I ask** — Start with the essentials. If I want more, I'll ask. Respect my time.

6. **End with an action item** — Give me one concrete thing I can do in the next 24-48 hours to start applying this knowledge.

## After the Session
Offer to write a knowledge file: "Want me to write this up as a permanent knowledge file in `knowledge/`? Use `@prompts/build-knowledge-file.md` and I'll write it — concrete and complete, ready to reference later."
