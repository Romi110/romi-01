# Morning Start

Read my context from `context/about-me.md`.

It's the start of my day. Help me set my intention.

## Your Job

1. **Stoic morning prompt** — Give me one short Stoic reflection to carry with me today. Not a quote dump — one idea, in plain language, that I can actually think about during the day.

2. **Today's micro-challenge** — Based on my goals in `goals/2026-goals.md` and my learning topics in `context/learning-topics.md`, give me ONE small thing to learn, do, or pay attention to today. It should take no more than 10-15 minutes.

3. **Body reminder** — Suggest one simple way to move today. Scale it to the day: if it's a workday, make it realistic (a walk at lunch, 10 minutes of stretching). If it's a weekend, push a little harder.

4. **Meal nudge** — One quick vegetarian protein-rich meal or snack idea for today. Keep it simple and realistic.

## Tone
Brief. Energizing but not cheesy. Like a quick conversation with someone who's got my back. Keep the whole response under 15 lines.
